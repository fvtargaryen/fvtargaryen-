## 👨‍🔬 About Me

🎓 **M.Sc. in Chemistry (Physical Chemistry)**  
Federal University of Pará (UFPA)

🔬 **Computer Modeling of Molecular Biosystems Group (CompMBio)**

I am a computational chemistry researcher specializing in molecular modeling, molecular dynamics simulations, and the development of Python-based workflows for biomolecular data analysis.

My research focuses on **protein–ligand interactions**, **computer-aided drug discovery**, **free-energy calculations**, and **multiscale computational methods** for studying biomolecular systems. I am also interested in the application of **carbon nanotubes as nanocarriers for drug delivery** and other nanobiotechnology applications.
